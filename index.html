COVID-19 Information Dashboard

India is currently facing one of the biggest challenges it has faced in its existence -- a pandemic that is ravaging the country. Among the biggest challenges people face is lack of information. Where do I go? Which hospitals have beds? Where can I procure oxygen? Where do I get Remdesivir? And a whole lot more.

It is not that information is unavailable. In fact, a great many people and organizations from across the country have created many websites and apps with information. However, the sheer number of these resources can itself make it hard to find the right one.

Here we have tried to identify and list all the information resources currently on the web. We have tried to organize it by city, where possible. Remember, these are often only starting points.

If you know of any other relevant links/leads please drop us an email on indiacovidresources10@gmail.com

We will be updating this page regularly with newer information as we receive it.

While we are attempting to verify the links, they have not all been verified, so the reader must assume that they are not verified.

Covid tests

A number of different types of tests available for COVID-19, including Rapid antigen test , RT-PCR , D-dimer , Ferritin , CRP , LDH , and HRCT. None of them are foolproof -- a person can be negative on all the tests, and still have Covid.
